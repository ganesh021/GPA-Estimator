About SPPU Pointer Estimator
'SPPU Pointer Estimator' is an reliable and effective tool developed for students studying Engineering at Savitribai Phule Pune University (SPPU). It can effectively help students to predict their pointer and accordingly plan their studies.

  • How Does it Work
  • In the first application the student can estimate minimum marks required in each subject to score his targeted pointer.The student can set his target pointer that he wishes to score by selecting his current Course Pattern, Branch and Semester. Next he is given an option to select difficuilty level for each subject, ranging in 3 steps easy, moderate, hard. The difficuilty level should be choosen relatively for each subject, based on personal inception towards a subject. According to the target pointer set and relative difficuilty preferences the algorithm gives you Minimum Marks required in each subject to achieve your target pointer. Hence here you need to relatively score more in subjects choosen as Hard and vice versa and finally your Target pointer remains intact and is optimally achieved.
  • In the second application you can enter your estimated marks one by one for each subject and predict corresponding pointer for your estimated marks. You have to simply enter marks which you think you can score in each subject and according to SPPU credit system your pointer and percentage corresponding to your pointer is calculated.
  • How is it Useful
  • Everybody have their own target for Pointer. For some it may be in the range of 9, for some in range of 8 or in range of 7. Here we calculate the Minimum marks which are required in each subject in order to achieve your Target Pointer. Additionally here we also factor for relative Difficuilty Preferences choosed by you for each subject.
  • Because of this you can manage your academics effectively by analyzing your need (Target Pointer) and accordingly plan your studies. And focus more on practical aspects of Engineering and Entrepreneurship to pursue your dreams.

How is it Useful

Everybody have their own target for Pointer. For someone it may be in the range of 9, for some in range of 8 or in range of 7. Here we calculate the Minimum marks which will be required in each subject in order to achieve your Target Pointer. Additionally we also factor in for Relative Difficuilty Preferences choosed by you for each subject.

Accordingly you can manage your academics effectively by analyzing your need (Target Pointer) and accordingly plan your studies. And thus focus more on practical aspects of Engineering and Entrepreneurship to pursue your dreams.
